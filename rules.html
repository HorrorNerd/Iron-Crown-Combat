Test Your Metal: The Crucible

Do you have what it takes to wear the Iron Crown? We are always searching for the next generation of elite fighters to prove their worth in The Crucible, our premier proving ground event.

Entry Requirements

  • Fighters must submit a complete profile, including their name, physical statistics, and a brief biography detailing their fighting style and background.
  • A clear, high-quality photo must be provided for promotional purposes.
  • All entrants must agree to the Iron Crown Combat code of conduct and athletic regulations.

How to Submit

To submit your fighter for consideration for an upcoming Crucible event, please send an email with all required information to our matchmaking department.

Contact: submissions@ironcrowncombat.com
